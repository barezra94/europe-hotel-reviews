Logging Information

Where to view logs: Logs are output to the terminal/console where the FastAPI server is running.

Log format: Structured logs include timestamp, level, message, request_id, query_text, retrieved_doc_count, grader_decision, and final_latency.

Example log entry: 
timestamp=2026-01-14 10:30:45 | level=INFO | message=Request completed | request_id=abc123 | query_text=Is wifi good? | retrieved_doc_count=5 | grader_decision=True | final_latency=1.234

Metrics update automatically. Click refresh to see latest values.
